Kale Emporium wants to help you eat better.

We live in a culture that prioritizes profit over people and where cooking meals for ourselves on a regular basis takes tremendous effort - The Kale Emporium is a volunteer organization that responds to these issues with weekly donation-based community dinners.

We seek to expand access to local produce, offer nutrition education, facilitate self-study, host culinary skillshares, support farmers financially, reduce food waste, and care for others through our cooking and the sense of community that comes with it.

Everyone deserves to eat a good meal.

We try to make it a little easier.
